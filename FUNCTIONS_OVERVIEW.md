# Tai lieu mo ta function cua du an ASM_AutoStock

## Pham vi quet

Tai lieu nay mo ta cac function/method chinh trong phan source cua du an:

- `src/ASM.WebPortal`
- `src/ASM.Infrastructure`
- `src/ASM.Domain`
- `tests/ASM.Tests`

Khong liet ke cac file sinh tu dong trong `bin/`, `obj/` va cac method autogenerated cua EF migration/designer.

## Tong quan kien truc

- `ASM.WebPortal`: giao dien MVC, controller xu ly luong dang nhap, dashboard, quan ly kho, don nhap/xuat va tac vu cho staff.
- `ASM.Infrastructure`: hien thuc nghiep vu, persistence, seed du lieu, QR, auth, dashboard.
- `ASM.Domain`: entity, enum, constant va mot so helper method don gian.
- `ASM.Application`: interface va DTO/contract. Cac interface tai `Interfaces.cs` phan chieu truc tiep cac service method o `Infrastructure`.

## 1. WebPortal

### `Program.cs`

- `AddControllersWithViews(...)`: dang ky MVC va them `SessionExpiredExceptionFilter`.
- `AddJsonOptions(...)`: serialize enum dang string.
- `AddSession(...)`: cau hinh session cho flow staff scan task.
- `AddInfrastructure(...)`: nap DbContext, Identity, JWT, DI cho cac service.
- `AddAuthentication(...).AddCookie(...)`: cau hinh dang nhap bang cookie cho portal.
- `MapControllers()`: map attribute routing.
- `MapControllerRoute(...)`: map route cho area va route mac dinh.
- `SeedAsync(...)`: apply migration va seed du lieu he thong/demo khi app start.

### `Controllers/AccountController.cs`

- `Login(string? returnUrl = null, bool expired = false)`: hien thi form dang nhap va thong bao neu session het han.
- `Login(LoginViewModel model, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: xac thuc user bang Identity, tao cookie claims va dieu huong ve trang phu hop theo role.
- `Logout()`: xoa session, sign out cookie roi quay ve trang login.
- `AccessDenied(string? returnUrl = null)`: tra ve view bao khong co quyen truy cap.
- `RedirectToRoleHome(string role)`: dieu huong admin ve khu admin, staff ve khu staff, cac role con lai ve dashboard.
- `IsAllowedReturnUrl(string? returnUrl, string role)`: kiem tra `returnUrl` co hop le va co dung pham vi role duoc phep truy cap hay khong.

### `Controllers/AdminController.cs`

- `Index(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tai dashboard admin va top owner hoat dong gan day.
- `Owners(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: hien thi danh sach owner cung thong ke su dung.
- `Owner(Guid id, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tai trang chi tiet mot owner kem du lieu form chinh sua.
- `UpdateOwner(AdminOwnerEditViewModel model, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: validate va cap nhat thong tin owner/tenant.

### `Controllers/DashboardController.cs`

- `Index(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tai dashboard cho owner/manager gom summary, inbound orders, outbound orders.

### `Controllers/ManagementController.cs`

- `Warehouses(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: hien thi danh sach kho va dam bao moi kho co QR tuong ung.
- `CreateWarehouse(WarehousesPageViewModel model, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao kho moi roi sinh QR cho kho do.

- `AddRack(LayoutPageViewModel model, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: them rack vao area va sinh QR cho rack.
- `AddSlot(LayoutPageViewModel model, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: them slot vao rack va sinh QR cho slot.
- `AddPallet(LayoutPageViewModel model, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao pallet cho kho va sinh QR cho pallet.
- `Products(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tai danh sach san pham va QR tuong ung.
- `CreateProduct(ProductsPageViewModel model, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao san pham roi sinh QR.
- `Pallets(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tai danh sach pallet, kho va QR cua pallet.
- `CreatePallet(PalletsPageViewModel model, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao pallet moi tu man quan ly pallet.
- `Users(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tai danh sach user trong tenant hien tai.
- `CreateUser(UsersPageViewModel model, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao tai khoan moi theo role duoc chon.
- `ToggleUser(Guid userId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: bat/tat trang thai active cua user.
- `Qr()`: mo man hinh tao QR thu cong.
- `GenerateQr(QrPageViewModel model, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: sinh QR tu thong tin nhap tay va tra ve view cung ket qua.
- `DownloadQr(Guid qrId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: render QR thanh file PNG de tai xuong.
- `EnsureQrsAsync<T>(...)`: helper private, duyet danh sach item va bao dam tung item co QR.

- `Outbound(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: mo trang tao/xem lenh xuat kho.
- `CreateOutbound(OutboundPageViewModel model, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao outbound order va task di kem.

- `BuildOutboundModelAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: dung view model cho man xuat kho, loc pallet da co vi tri hop le de pick.
- `LoadLayoutsAsync(...)`: nap layout cua tung kho vao dictionary.

- `BuildPalletOptions(...)`: chuyen danh sach pallet thanh option hien thi warehouse/path/status.
- `BuildSlotPathLookup(...)`: tao map `slotId -> duong dan day du`.

### `Areas/Staff/Controllers/TasksController.cs`

- `Index(string? status, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tai danh sach task cua staff va loc theo trang thai.
- `Details(Guid id, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: hien thi chi tiet task va order lien quan.
- `Scan(Guid id, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: mo man hinh thuc thi task tung buoc.
- `Start(Guid id)`: danh dau staff da bat dau task va luu progress vao session.
- `Verify(Guid id, string stepKey, string? manualPayload, string? cameraPayload, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: xac minh QR theo tung step cua task.
- `Complete(Guid id, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: hoan tat inbound/outbound task va cap nhat ton kho thuc te.
- `BuildExecutionViewModelAsync(...)`: tai du lieu order/task de dung man hinh scan tung buoc.
- `BuildSteps(...)`: tao danh sach step hien thi tuy theo task inbound hay outbound.
- `GetProgressState(Guid taskId)`: doc trang thai thuc thi task tu session.

- `ClearProgressState(Guid taskId)`: xoa progress da luu khi hoan tat task.
- `BuildProgressKey(Guid taskId)`: tao session key chuan cho moi task.

### `Services/SessionExpiredExceptionFilter.cs`

### `Areas/Staff/Models/TaskViewModels.cs`

## 2. Infrastructure

### `Configuration/ServiceCollectionExtensions.cs`

- `AddInfrastructure(this IServiceCollection services, IConfiguration configuration)`: dang ky JWT options, DbContext SQL Server, Identity, auth/authorization va toan bo service cua he thong.
- `SeedAsync(this IServiceProvider serviceProvider, bool applyMigrationsOnStartup = true, bool seedSystemData = true, bool seedDemoData = true)`: apply migration, tao role, tenant, account mac dinh va du lieu demo ban dau.
- `EnsureUserAsync(...)`: helper private de tao moi hoac cap nhat user seed, roi gan role phu hop.

### `Persistence/AppDbContext.cs`

- `AppDbContext(DbContextOptions<AppDbContext> options)`: khoi tao DbContext.
- `OnModelCreating(ModelBuilder builder)`: cau hinh index, quan he, foreign key va delete behavior cho toan bo entity.

### `Services/CurrentUserService.cs`

- `IsInRole(string role)`: kiem tra user hien tai co thuoc role truyen vao hay khong.
- `GetGuid(string claimType)`: helper private de parse claim string thanh `Guid`.

### `Services/AuthService.cs`

- `LoginAsync(LoginRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: dang nhap bang username/password va phat access token + refresh token.
- `RefreshAsync(RefreshTokenRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: revoke refresh token cu va phat bo token moi.
- `LogoutAsync(string refreshToken, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: revoke refresh token hien tai.
- `GetCurrentUserAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tra ve thong tin user hien tai dua tren claims.
- `IssueTokensAsync(AppUser user, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: helper private tao JWT, refresh token va luu refresh token vao DB.

### `Services/WarehouseService.cs`

- `GetWarehousesAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay danh sach kho theo tenant hien tai.
- `CreateWarehouseAsync(CreateWarehouseRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao kho moi va ghi audit log.
- `AddAreaAsync(CreateAreaRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: them area vao kho.
- `AddRackAsync(CreateRackRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: them rack vao area.
- `AddSlotAsync(CreateSlotRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: them slot vao rack, mac dinh `IsOccupied = false`.
- `GetLayoutAsync(Guid warehouseId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tra ve layout day du cua kho gom area, rack, slot.
- `EnsureWarehouseInTenantAsync(Guid warehouseId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: kiem tra kho co thuoc tenant hien tai khong.
- `SaveAuditAsync(...)`: helper private ghi audit log va `SaveChanges`.

### `Services/CatalogService.cs`

- `GetProductsAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay danh sach san pham trong tenant.
- `CreateProductAsync(CreateProductRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao san pham moi va ghi audit.
- `GetPalletsAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay danh sach pallet kem inventory item va product tuong ung.
- `CreatePalletAsync(CreatePalletRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao pallet trong kho hop le, mac dinh trang thai `Empty`.
- `MapPallet(Pallet pallet)`: helper private map entity pallet sang `PalletDto`.
- `SaveAuditAsync(...)`: helper private ghi audit log cho thao tac catalog.

### `Services/QrService.cs`

- `GenerateAsync(CreateQrRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao QR moi theo target type/id, hoac tra lai QR cu neu da ton tai.
- `GetAsync(Guid qrId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay thong tin mot QR theo id.
- `RenderPngAsync(Guid qrId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: render QR payload thanh anh PNG bang thu vien `QRCoder`.
- `Map(QrCode qr)`: helper private map entity QR sang DTO.

### `Services/UserService.cs`

- `GetUsersAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay danh sach user trong tenant kem role hien tai.
- `CreateUserAsync(CreateUserRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao user moi, validate role va chi cho admin tao admin khac.
- `ToggleUserStatusAsync(Guid userId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: dao trang thai `IsActive` cua user.
- `AddAuditAsync(...)`: helper private ghi audit log cho thao tac quan ly user.

### `Services/DashboardService.cs`

- `GetSummaryAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tong hop so kho, san pham, pallet dang chua hang, so lenh chua hoan tat, task pending va danh sach canh bao scan loi.

### `Services/AdminService.cs`

- `GetDashboardAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tong hop dashboard cap nen tang cho admin, gom owner active, so order, scan log va alerts.
- `GetOwnersAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: xay bao cao su dung cho tung owner va tenant cua owner do.
- `GetOwnerDetailsAsync(Guid ownerUserId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay summary, hoat dong gan day va frequency chart cua mot owner.
- `GetOwnerEditAsync(Guid ownerUserId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay du lieu owner phuc vu form edit.
- `UpdateOwnerAsync(UpdateOwnerProfileRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: cap nhat thong tin owner, tenant va ghi audit log.
- `EnsureAdmin()`: helper private chan truy cap neu user hien tai khong phai admin he thong.

### `Services/OrderService.cs`

- `GetInboundOrdersAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay danh sach inbound order cua tenant, kem warehouse, staff, line va task.
- `GetInboundOrderAsync(Guid orderId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay chi tiet mot inbound order va kiem tra quyen staff duoc gan.
- `CreateInboundOrderAsync(CreateInboundOrderRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao lenh nhap kho, line nhap kho va task assignment tuong ung.
- `CompleteInboundOrderAsync(Guid orderId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: hoan tat lenh nhap, dat pallet vao slot, cap nhat ton kho va trang thai task/order.
- `GetOutboundOrdersAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay danh sach outbound order cua tenant.
- `GetOutboundOrderAsync(Guid orderId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay chi tiet mot outbound order va kiem tra quyen staff duoc gan.
- `CreateOutboundOrderAsync(CreateOutboundOrderRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tao lenh xuat kho, kiem tra pallet/slot/inventory roi sinh task pick hang.
- `CompleteOutboundOrderAsync(Guid orderId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: tru ton kho tren pallet, xoa inventory item neu het so luong va cap nhat trang thai order/task.
- `GetMyTasksAsync(CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay danh sach task theo tenant, neu la staff thi chi lay task duoc assign cho chinh minh.
- `GetTaskAsync(Guid taskId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: lay chi tiet mot task va kiem tra quyen truy cap.
- `VerifyScanAsync(ScanVerifyRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: xac minh payload QR theo `ExpectedTargetType/ExpectedTargetId` cua task kieu cu mot buoc.
- `VerifyTaskStepAsync(TaskStepVerifyRequest request, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: xac minh QR theo tung step cua flow task moi nhieu buoc.
- `EnsureTenantEntityAsync<T>(Guid id, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: helper generic kiem tra entity thuoc tenant hien tai.
- `EnsureAssignedStaffAsync(Guid userId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: bao dam user duoc assign ton tai, active va cung tenant.
- `ResolveStepExpectationAsync(TaskAssignment task, string stepKey, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: suy ra step hien tai can quet loai QR nao, id nao, message thanh cong/that bai va buoc ke tiep.
- `EnsureTaskCompletable(TaskAssignment? task)`: kiem tra task da duoc verify thanh cong truoc khi complete chua.
- `LoadInboundOrderAsync(Guid orderId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: helper private nap inbound order voi day du navigation can thiet.
- `LoadOutboundOrderAsync(Guid orderId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: helper private nap outbound order voi day du navigation can thiet.
- `LoadSlotWithHierarchyAsync(Guid slotId, CancellationToken cancellationToken)`: nap slot cung rack/area de xac minh warehouse path.
- `SaveAuditAsync(...)`: helper private ghi audit log va `SaveChanges`.
- `MapInbound(InboundOrder order)`: map entity inbound order sang DTO.
- `MapOutbound(OutboundOrder order)`: map entity outbound order sang DTO.
- `MapTask(TaskAssignment task)`: map task entity sang `TaskDto`.
- `EnsurePalletBelongsToWarehouse(Pallet pallet, Guid warehouseId)`: kiem tra pallet co thuoc kho da chon khong.
- `EnsureSlotBelongsToWarehouse(Slot slot, Guid warehouseId)`: kiem tra slot co nam trong kho da chon khong.
- `BuildPalletLocationPath(string warehouseName, Pallet? pallet)`: dung path vi tri hien tai cua pallet de hien thi.
- `BuildSlotPath(string warehouseName, Slot? slot)`: dung path `warehouse > area > rack > slot`.

## 5. Interface trong `ASM.Application`

File `src/ASM.Application/Interfaces/Interfaces.cs` dinh nghia cac interface sau:

- `ICurrentUserService`
- `IAuthService`
- `IUserService`
- `IWarehouseService`
- `ICatalogService`
- `IQrService`
- `IOrderService`
- `IDashboardService`
- `IAdminService`

Cac method trong interface nay tuong ung gan nhu 1-1 voi cac method public da duoc mo ta o phan `Infrastructure Services`.

## Goi y doc nhanh theo nghiep vu

- Dang nhap va phan quyen: `AccountController`, `AuthService`, `CurrentUserService`
- Quan ly kho/cau truc kho: `ManagementController`, `WarehouseService`
- San pham, pallet, QR: `CatalogService`, `QrService`
- Nhap kho/xuat kho/task scan: `OrdersController`, `TasksController`, `OrderService`
- Dashboard tenant: `DashboardService`, `DashboardController`
- Dashboard he thong/admin: `AdminController`, `AdminService`
